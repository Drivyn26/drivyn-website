FAQ

Questions We Get Every Week

If you're thinking it, someone else already asked it.

How much does it cost?+
Our systems start from $1,500 for a standalone website, up to full revenue system packages. The better question is: what's a booked job worth to you? Most clients recover the investment within the first 30–60 days of going live. Every engagement starts with a free audit so you know exactly what you're getting before committing.
How long does it take to go live?+
Most builds go live within 7–10 days. We move fast deliberately — every day your current setup is losing you leads. Once we have your content and onboarding info, we build quickly and keep you in the loop throughout.
What kind of results can I realistically expect?+
Every business is different, but our clients typically see a meaningful increase in inbound calls within the first 30 days, and a significant jump in booked jobs by day 60–90. The biggest wins usually come from the AI follow-up and missed call systems — these often generate ROI in the first week alone.
Do I need to be tech-savvy?+
Not at all. We handle the entire build, setup, and launch. Once everything is ready, we do a 25-minute walkthrough call — we show you your system and send over your credentials. That's it. After that, most of it runs on its own. Our clients are busy tradespeople and business owners — not tech people. That's exactly the point.
I already have a website. Can you work with that?+
We'll audit it first. If it's already converting well, we'll layer our AI and automation systems on top. If it's not converting (most aren't), we'll show you the data and recommend a rebuild. Either way, you leave the audit with clear answers.
Is there ongoing support after launch?+
Yes — every client gets ongoing support directly from your Project Manager and our specialist. Monthly performance reports, optimisations based on real data, and real people who actually pick up. We build long-term relationships with the businesses we work with — not support tickets.
